About

Professor and Faculty Co-Director of Wharton Human-AI Research

Prasanna (Sonny) Tambe is a Professor in the OID group at the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania and Faculty Co-Director of Wharton Human-AI Research.

His research examines the economics of technical labor markets, how AI affects work and skills, and how algorithms and data signals are used in human resources.
